Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

You earn points by completing assigned tasks such as liking posts, voting, and referring friends.

How much is 1000 points worth?

1000 points equals ₦5,000 which you can withdraw once you reach the threshold.

Can I refer people?

Yes! You earn 10 points each time someone signs up with your referral code.

How do I cash out?

Once you hit 1000 points, the "Cash Out" button activates, and you can request withdrawal directly from your dashboard.

Do I need to pay to register?

No. Registration on Daily Tasks is completely free.

How often are new tasks added?

New tasks are added daily. Be sure to check your dashboard regularly for updates.

What happens after I upload evidence?

Our system will verify your submission, and points will be credited automatically if valid.

How long does task approval take?

Most tasks are verified instantly. However, manual reviews may take up to 24 hours.

Can I withdraw below 1000 points?

No. The minimum withdrawal threshold is 1000 points (₦5,000).

Where can I see my total points?

Your current points are displayed on the dashboard under the “Earnings” section.

What if my referral didn't use my code?

Referrals must register using your unique code for you to receive the bonus points.

Can I edit my profile information?

Yes. Navigate to your profile section to update your details such as name, email, or password.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form or whatsapp on the site.

Is there a referral limit?

No. You can refer as many friends as you like and earn unlimited referral bonuses.

What devices can I use?

Daily Tasks is optimized for all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile phones.
