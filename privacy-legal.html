Privacy & Legal

Introduction

Welcome to Daily Tasks (https://dailytasks.co), a Nigerian-based platform where users earn points by completing tasks, including surveys from partnered platforms like Toluna. We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users and the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) for local users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal data:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, age, contact details, location, and user ID when you register or log in.
  • Usage Data: Activities on the platform (e.g., tasks completed, points earned).
  • Survey Data: Information you provide during surveys, collected by our partnered survey platforms.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services, including task delivery and point tracking.
  • To facilitate partnerships with survey platforms, enabling them to offer you surveys and reward you accordingly.
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations (e.g., GDPR, NDPR).

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: You agree by submitting the registration form.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our platform and prevent fraud, balanced with your rights.
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our service agreement with you.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with:

  • Survey Partners: Third-party survey platforms to deliver surveys and process rewards. These partners may collect additional data as per their privacy policies.
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and authentication measures to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR (for EU users) and NDPR (for Nigerian users), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion (subject to legal exceptions).
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Right to Object: Oppose certain processing (e.g., marketing).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out at any time by contacting us.

To exercise these rights, email dailytasksoffice@gmail.com or update your settings.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Survey partners may also use tracking technologies, which you can manage via browser settings.

Eligibility & Age Requirements

To use Daily Tasks, you must be at least 18 years old, or the age of majority in your country of residence if it is higher. In some jurisdictions (such as certain EU countries under GDPR), the minimum age for online consent may be 16. We require all users to confirm their age at registration. Accounts that do not meet the minimum age requirement are not permitted to participate, and any attempt to misrepresent age may result in suspension or termination of the account.

International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in Nigeria or by partners in the EU/US, adhering to GDPR standards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) and NDPR.
