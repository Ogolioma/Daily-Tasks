Privacy & Legal

Introduction


Welcome to Daily Tasks (https://dailytasks.co), a platform where users can earn points by completing tasks, including surveys provided by partnered survey platforms.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data.


Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Data: Name, email address, age, contact details, location, and user ID when you register or log in.

Usage Data: Activities on the platform (e.g., tasks completed, points earned) and device information (e.g., IP address, browser type).

Survey Data: Information you provide during surveys, collected by our partnered survey platforms.


How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services, including task delivery and point tracking.
  • To facilitate partnerships with survey platforms, enabling them to offer you surveys and reward you accordingly.
  • To ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
